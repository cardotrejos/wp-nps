Recommendation for FlowPulse

Based on Derek's "30-second pulse check" use case and the emotional goals of "calm" and "in control," Direction 1 (Hero Pulse) or Direction 4 (Mobile Native) are the strongest fits. They maximize glanceability while maintaining a professional, calm aesthetic.

Consider elements from Direction 2 (WhatsApp Native) for the response cards to reinforce the WhatsApp positioning.
